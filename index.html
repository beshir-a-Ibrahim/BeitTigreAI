üìö Overview

The Tigre language, spoken in Eritrea and eastern Sudan, is the closest living relative to Classical Geez. While Geez is no longer spoken, Tigre remains vibrant, with content on:

  • Wikipedia
  • Common Voice
  • Tatoeba
  • Radio, TV, newspapers

Tigre is underrepresented in NLP. This project aims to close the gap with open data and models. Its closeness to Geez also makes it a bridge to understanding that classical language.
